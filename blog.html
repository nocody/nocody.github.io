June 10, 2021

Welcome. Why are you here? I don't have much to offer. I'm not really interested in building an audience or participating in some broader community. What I'm looking for here is the illusion of accountability.

I hope this will be sort of like a journal with higher standards of clarity and coherence.

What's my deal? I'm a computer science student staring down graduation.

At this stage, I find myself asking a lot of questions. What I have gotten out of my education thus far? How has my decision to go back to school improved what I have to offer?

I want to develop a more meaningful demonstration of my knowledge and skillsets; a body of work more authentic to my abilities and passion than what I have been obligated to create in school.

My first endevour in this project is the creation of my portfolio and this blog.

Next, I will probably try to bring one of my many half finished side projects and experiments to completion. Whatever it is, I'll be writting about it here.
